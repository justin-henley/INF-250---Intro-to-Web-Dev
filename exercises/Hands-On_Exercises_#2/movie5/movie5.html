Up (2009)


Director:
Pete Docter
Leading Actor:
Ed Asner
Leading Actress:
Elizabeth Docter
Rating:
98% Rotten Tomatoes

After the loss of his life-long partner, Carl Fredricksen decides to make good on an old promise to take their house to South America to live in their dream location, Paradise Falls. Russel, a young Wildnerness Explorer deteermined to get his badge for "Assisting the Elderly," becomes an accidental stowaway on the front porch after the house takes flight. After a storm carries them all the way to South America, they begin an arduous journey full of dogs, evil childhood heros, and snipes.

"Up is buoyant with thrills and spills, yet it's anchored, quite movingly, in the acceptance of mortality. This rollercoaster ride will leave everyone on an up."

— Anthony Quinn, Independent (UK)
