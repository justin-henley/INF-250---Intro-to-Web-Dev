Cycle Touring

Cycle touring is the sport of long-distance, long-term cycling. Substituting human power for horsepower, sadists cyclists travel cross-country on their bicycles, often camping and cooking along the way with all their equipment strapped to their bikes. A form of slow travel, it allows riders to really get to know a place, rather than speeding through on trains or planes.

Japan 2018

After quiting our jobs as teachers in 2018, my fiancee and I flew to Sendai, Japan to begin our round-the-world trip. We cycled the east coast of Japan's main island of Honshu, as well as a trip across the mountainous island of Shikoku.

Camping our way through rice paddies, mountains, and cities, for two months we lived on our bikes and visited some of the most popular sites in Japan. Ready for some socialization and rest, we spent the next month in Osaka working in a hostel for travelers.

Spain 2018

After our month in Osaka, we decided to see Europe next. Riding under-equipped after our suitcase full of gear went missing, we hopped from hotel to hostel from Madrid to Barcelona.

A long, narrow mountain highway climb destroyed my knee after 40 miles with no shoulder on which to stop and rest. This spelled the end of the cycling part of our trip, but I have recently bought a new bike and started reconditioning my knee.
